Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for, and captains, both Saudi Pro League club Al-Nassr and the Portugal national team. Nicknamed CR7, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history, and has won numerous individual accolades throughout his career, including five Ballon d'Ors, a record three UEFA Men's Player of the Year Awards, four European Golden Shoes, and was named five times the world's best player by FIFA.

He has won 34 trophies in his career, including five UEFA Champions Leagues and the UEFA European Championship. He holds the records for most goals (140) and assists (42) in the Champions League, goals (14) and assists (8) in the European Championship, and most international appearances (226) and international goals (143). He is the only player to have scored 100 goals with four different clubs. He has made over 1,300 professional career appearances, the most by an outfield player, and has scored over 950 official senior career goals for club and country, making him the top goalscorer of all time. Timeline: Cristiano Ronaldo

  1. Early Years and Career Beginnings

    • 1985 (February 5) – Born in Funchal, Madeira (Portugal).
    • 1997 – Leaves Madeira at age 12 to join the Sporting CP football academy in Lisbon
    • 2002 – Makes his professional debut for Sporting CP's first team (scoring two goals in his league debut).

  2. First Manchester United Era

    • 2003 (August) – Signs with Manchester United after an impressive performance in a friendly match against them. He receives the legendary number 7 shirt.
    • 2004 – Reaches the Euro 2004 final with the Portuguese national team (losing to Greece).
    • 2008 – Wins the UEFA Champions League for the first time and receives his first Ballon d'Or for the best player in the world.

  3. Real Madrid Era (Career Peak)

    • 2009 – Transfers to Real Madrid for a then-world-record fee of €94 million.
    • 2011 – Wins the European Golden Shoe (the first of four in his career).
    • 2013–2014 – Wins his second Ballon d'Or and the Champions League with Real Madrid (the famous "La Decima").
    • 2016 – Wins the UEFA European Championship (Euro 2016) with Portugal – his country's first major trophy.
    • 2016–2017 – Further triumphs in the Champions League and subsequent Ballon d'Or awards (bringing his total to 5).
    • 2018 (May) – Wins the Champions League for the third consecutive time (his fifth title overall).

  4. Juventus, Return to England, and Saudi Arabia

    • 2018 (July) – Transfers to Juventus (Italy).
    • 2019 – Wins the UEFA Nations League with Portugal.
    • 2021 (September) – Breaks Ali Daei's record for the most goals scored in men's international football (becoming the all-time leading international scorer).
    • 2021 (August) – Sensational return to Manchester United.
    • 2022 (November) – Contract with Manchester United is terminated by mutual agreement following a controversial interview, just before the World Cup.
    • 2022 (December) – Signs a contract with the Saudi Arabian club Al-Nassr.
    • 2023 – Official debut in the Saudi Pro League; becomes the highest-paid footballer in history.
    • 2024 – Surpasses the 900-goal mark in official matches (club and country combined).
